PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated August 05, 2025

This Privacy Notice for Marklie B.V. ("we," "us," or "our") describes how and why we may collect, store, use, and/or share ("process") your personal information when you use our services ("Services"), including when you:

  • Visit our website at marklie.com or any other site that links to this Privacy Notice
  • Use our mobile or web applications
  • Interact with us in other ways, such as through customer support, events, or marketing

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at info@marklie.com.

1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

We collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Personal Identifiers – Such as your name, email address, phone number, billing address, and account credentials.
  • Sensitive Information – Including login credentials and debit/credit card numbers, processed securely through Stripe.
  • Integration Data – If you connect third-party accounts (e.g., Facebook, TikTok), we access relevant data from those platforms via authorized APIs to generate client reports. This data is limited to scopes you explicitly grant and may be cached temporarily.

2. HOW DO WE USE YOUR INFORMATION?

We use your personal information to:

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Deliver requested services and respond to inquiries
  • Communicate with you, including sending administrative and promotional emails
  • Monitor and improve service performance and user experience
  • Detect and prevent security threats and fraudulent activities
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. COOKIES AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES

We use cookies to enhance your experience, including:

  • Session Cookies – To maintain login sessions

Blocking cookies may affect functionality.

4. SHARING YOUR INFORMATION

We may share your information with third-party service providers who support our operations. These include:

  • Stripe – For secure payment processing
  • Email and messaging services (e.g., SendGrid, Mailgun)

All partners are contractually required to handle your data responsibly and only for specified purposes.

We do not sell your personal data.

5. DO-NOT-TRACK DISCLOSURE

Our website does not respond to Do-Not-Track (DNT) signals. There is currently no industry standard for recognizing or honoring DNT browser signals.

6. YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES

You may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccuracies in your data
  • Delete your personal data
  • Withdraw consent or restrict processing
  • Object to certain types of processing, such as targeted advertising
  • Request a portable copy of your data

To exercise your rights, contact us at info@marklie.com or submit a data subject access request.

We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling certain requests.

7. SECURITY OF YOUR INFORMATION

We use industry-standard technical and organizational safeguards to protect your personal data. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. You use the Services at your own risk.

8. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date and notify you if changes are significant. Continued use of the Services after updates constitutes your agreement to the revised policy.

9. CONTACT US

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us at:

Email: info@marklie.com
